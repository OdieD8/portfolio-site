About

OD-Werks is Odie de Moura's brand. All his applications and services fall under this brand.



Odie is a software developer with a business background in the print, supply chain, and logistics industries. He specializes in web development utilizing the MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express.JS, Angular.JS, Node.JS) and desktop development in C# and SQL.

This brand OD-WERKS has come about out of Odie's love of software development: his desire to experiment with new technologies, bringing app ideas to life, and a programming hobby he enjoys when not at work

Odie is open to freelance development opportunities and teaching new developers how to code. Please contact him to learn more.
